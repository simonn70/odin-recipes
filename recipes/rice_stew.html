Rice and Stew Recipe

The Ghanaian rice and stew

The Ghanaian rice and stew recipe is quite an easy and universal dish to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of long grain rice
  • 1 kg meat, sliced
  • 1 round ball of garlic
  • 1 cup beef stock
  • three tablespoon of tomato paste
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 3 beef cubes
  • 1 teaspoon of curry powder
  • 1/2 cup of gino oil
  • 2 medium sized onions

steps

  1. Add your garlic and ginger paste to the meat. Stir until it coats the meat. Add water and bring to a boil.
  2. Soak your long grain rice for 15 minutes.
  3. Once the water has evaporated in the pan with the meat, add your extra virgin oil and fry until they turn crispy brown.
  4. Add your chopped onions to the fried meat and stir slowly
  5. Add your curry powder, cumin, beef cubes and turmeric powder. Cover the pan and let the mixture simmer for 2 minutes.
  6. Add your diced tomatoes to the mixture. Add the tomato paste to the mixture. Add a little water to prevent the mixture from coating at the bottom of the pan.
  7. Cover the mixture and let it simmer for 5 minutes
  8. Add the beef stock to your desired thickness of the soup.
  9. Remove from the heat and set aside.
  10. Add 4 cups of water to a pan. Add salt and a little extra virgin oil.
